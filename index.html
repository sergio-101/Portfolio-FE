Won J. You is a seasoned design leader and educator who’s spent over 20 years designing and creating award-winning experiences. At WJY Studios, he leads the charge as chief disruptor and designer for social impact.

In his past, Won has led innovation efforts as design director at Discovery Communications, where he successfully incubated, patented, and launched products such as curiosity.com. More recently, he’s directed experience design work for Porsche, Edward Jones, Alzheimer’s Association, Bic, Cedar Fair, among others.

One of his proudest achievements has been educating hundreds of students while Chief Product Officer and curriculum director at Designation, one of the first design bootcamps in the country.

His students have gone on to work for Apple, Google, Meta, IBM Design, Capital One, as well as many other Fortune 500 companies.
